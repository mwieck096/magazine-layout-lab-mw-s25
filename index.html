Cat Chronicles: Kitten Facts

Kittens Need Lots of Sleep

Kittens need a lot of sleep to aid their development, make sure they get their sleep!

Born Deaf and Blind

Kittens are born with their eyes and ears sealed, and they begin to open up at around 1 week.

Biscuit Making

"Making biscuits" or kneading with their feet is how kittens nurse properly.
